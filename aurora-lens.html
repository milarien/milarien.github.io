01 — The gap

Why this asset matters

Aurora Lens addresses a structural gap in deployed AI systems.

Benchmarks, red-teaming, and monitoring can reveal risk. They do not govern what the model is permitted to say at the moment of output. Aurora Lens does.

This matters wherever unsupported or illegitimate output creates operational, legal, regulatory, or safety exposure.

02 — Distinction

What makes Aurora Lens different

Aurora Lens is not a benchmark, not a dashboard, and not a prompt wrapper.

It was built to address a persistent failure in deployed language systems: fluent output presented as legitimate knowledge under uncertainty, ambiguity, or missing evidence.

  • 01
    Deterministic admissibility enforcement Same input, same enforcement outcome. Not probabilistic scoring.
  • 02
    Refusal as a valid terminal outcome Non-answers are governed correct outcomes, not system failures.
  • 03
    Explicit handling of ambiguity and insufficient evidence The system names what it cannot resolve, rather than collapsing to a guess.
  • 04
    Structured intervention outcomes Every enforcement action is typed, recorded, and traceable.
  • 05
    Forensic audit records for governed inference Hash-chained, HMAC-sealed, tamper-evident by construction. Suitable for regulatory review.
  • 06
    Model-agnostic deployment surface Works in front of any existing LLM API. No model retraining. No SDK changes.
