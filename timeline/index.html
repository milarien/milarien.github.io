Public Timeline

The dated public chronology of the Aurora / Lens work from formal filing through public packaging and live deployment.

It begins at the point where the work entered externally legible, dateable record. The underlying research and architectural development predate these filings, but this timeline is limited to milestones that can be publicly anchored.
