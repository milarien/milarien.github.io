And finally - this is a view over the City of Melbourne itself.  The Central Business District is on the right and the Southbank Complex is on the left.  Southbank has become very much the place to be! particularly on the weekends.  It's a lovely shopping and eating area, within walking distance of the National Gallery and Flinders St Station - the main train station.  The city itself has several parks worth wandering through, and the top end of Collins Street is a lovely area.
